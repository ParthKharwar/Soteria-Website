What are Soteria Protection Partners

With our goal to make you feel safer everywhere you go, we have partnered with a wide range of verified restuartants, bars, and clubs to provide you with assistance should you feel unsafe. Each partner must meet a minimum set of safety standards to be verified. You can find a complete list of verified protection partners below or in our app.

If you are using our app and are at one of our protection partners, you can trigger the Assistance button to send your information to the staff who will then assist you and ensure you feel safe.

We follow a strict rating system where our users must first verify a partner before they can be added to our list of partners. Users can also report a location, with 3 strikes leading to a permanent blacklist from our partners.
