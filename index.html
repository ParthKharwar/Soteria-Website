Welcome to Soteria

Our goal is to make it easier for you to feel safer and protect yourself, no matter where you are and where you go. We provide a wide range of services to help you protect yourself and our network of protectees and protection partners will make you feel safe so that you can go out and rest assured that you are safe.

Soteria was the Greek goddess or spirit of safety and salvation, deliverance, and preservation from harm. We aim to embody her values in our services and protect you from the dangers of the world.
