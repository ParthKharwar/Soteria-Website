Register for Soteria

Registering for Soteria allows you to sign up to be a protectee and assist other women in need. You will only have access to the Forum and will receive emergency alerts at the phone number you provide. You can download our app from the Google Play Store or Apple Store and sign in with these same credentials to access all the features of Soteria

Already have an account? Login instead!

What you'll need

  • Personal Information: Your name, date of birth, gender.
  • Email Address: To contact you in case of updates and emergencies.
  • Phone Number: Your phone number will be used to send you emergency alerts.
  • Address: Your address will be used to check if you are within range of someone in trouble.
  • Valid ID with a Picture: This will be used to verify your account.
